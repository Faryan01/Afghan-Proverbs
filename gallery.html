Gallery of Wisdom

باد آورده را باد می‌برد

What comes with the wind, goes with the wind

Easy gains are easily lost.

سنگ مفت، گنجشک مفت

Free stone, free sparrow

Nothing to lose in trying.

یک دست صدا ندارد

One hand alone makes no sound

Teamwork and unity are essential for success.

چشم بینا دل دانا

Seeing eye, wise heart

Wisdom is both in perception and emotion.

آب که یک‌جا بماند می‌گندد

Still water turns foul

Lack of movement leads to decay.

دو صد گفته چون نیم کردار نیست

Two hundred words are not worth half an action

Actions speak louder than words.

تا نباشد چیزکی مردم نگویند چیزها

There's no smoke without fire

Rumors often have a basis in truth.

از دل برود هر آنکه از دیده برفت

Out of sight, out of mind

Distance weakens emotional attachment.
